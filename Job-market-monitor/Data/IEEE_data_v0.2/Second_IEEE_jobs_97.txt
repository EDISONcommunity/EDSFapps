We are seeking candidates with strong Systems Engineering and/or Information Technology skills to enhance our nation's intelligence capabilities.  Candidates should possess excellent technical and communications skills and demonstrated ability to interact with MITRE and Government personnel at all levels.  
 
Position requires foundational experience with IT, cloud, DevOps, requirements analysis, data modeling, lifecycle planning and risk analysis and management.  You will be part of a talented team of engineers that demonstrate superb technical competency, delivering mission critical infrastructure and systems, while ensuring the highest levels of availability, performance and security.
 
Responsibilities
Identify technical requirements to satisfy functional needs
Architect enterprise, system and services solutions
Migrate legacy applications into the cloud
Develop and monitor processes and systems for operations and utilization
Oversee the development and installation of new hardware and software
Evaluate technology for suitability in customer environment
Propose solutions to customers hard IT problems 










Requirements:
BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject
Solid Cloud experience, preferably in AWS
Experience with automation software (e.g., Puppet, cfengine, Chef)
Solid interpersonal and communication skills
Able to translate technical topics into non technical terms
Able to interact with senior leadership government personnel
