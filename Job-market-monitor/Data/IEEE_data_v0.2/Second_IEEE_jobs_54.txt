MITRE's Netcentric Infrastructure group is looking for a strong strategic and technical leader with proven success in applying technology to impact warfighter missions.  The successful candidate will be a Division level staff serving as the senior technical staff to provide technical leadership across the three Division focused areas: Nuclear Command and Control, Communications (NC3), Global Tactical Communications, and Cyber Resilient Weapon Systems, with the initial emphasis on contributing to NC2 Communications.
 
Key functions: 
Contribute to the thought leadership and technical work program for the NC2 Communications System Engineering Project to contribute to the AF NC3 Weapon System and the NC3 Enterprise-          Develop future NC3 vision, technology roadmap
-          Conduct technology assessments critical to the success of the Nuclear Deterrence Operation
-          Identify Innovative solutions to drastically improve warfighter capability and mitigate future threats
-          Execute analysis, modeling and simulation, or experiments to provide data-driven recommendation to top DoD decision makers to build the future NC3 Enterprise
-          Implement recommended solution and field capability to warfighters
-          As sponsors needs change, apply the above to the other Division focus areas

Challenge the current thinking of our sponsors and our own team to make us all better
As part of the Division leadership team, advance sponsor mission through technical contributions (Deliver deep technical work)-          Understand Division strategy and shape division technical posture

-          Plan work to contribute and be the thought leaders to the other two Division focus areas: Global Tactical Communications and Cyber Resilient Weapon Systems
