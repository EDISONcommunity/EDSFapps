This position is an exciting opportunity for a candidate to have technical impact on future space capabilities.  
Serve as the lead Subject Matter expert (SME) and trusted advisor to Air Force Sponsors for Enterprise Software and Platforms.  
Use their expertise in software development and systems engineering (SE) to design and influence space capabilities and architectures.  
Lead a team of engineers towards this goal and work with other SMEs developing cyber services in support of the Enterprise Architecture.
Work with a multi-organization engineering team to design, test, and provide oversight to modern software development and designs in support of space technologies.  
Provide technical advice and lead technical analysis teams to this end. 
Lead customer engagement to include definition of technical needs and cutting edge internal research definition.  
 










Minimum Qualifications:
Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering or similar field and 8 years of experience. 
Demonstrates an ability to apply advanced principles, theories and concepts comparable to those obtained with a Master's degree in a relevant field of study. 
Understand the missions, domains and challenges of MITRE customers and is fully responsible for deliverables-including definition, completion, and technical quality, as well as assuring consistency across the responsibility span. 
Applicants selected for this position will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information or applicants who are eligible for security clearances.Required Qualifications:
Strong background in software development and systems engineering (SE) including, and not limited to: modern software development process and practice including agile development (e.g., DevOps), hands on experience with software architecture, design, and implementation with an emphasis on service oriented architecture (SOA), Enterprise Service Bus (ESB), and Enterprise Relational Databases including data and object modeling, schema development and database leveraged software development (e.g., Oracle11g/12 Enterprise Database and Oracle WebLogic Application Server). 
Considerable experience with object oriented design/implementation and services based design/implementation.  
Extensive experience using Java, Java EE/J2EE, C/C++, Python and current software development tools including, but not limited to: GIT, SVN, Clearcase for SCM/CM, Jenkins/Jira for development pipeline and test automation. 
Obtain and maintain a Top Secret/SCI level security clearancePreferred Qualifications:
Cyber experience and some background in Information Assurance (IA) including government STIGs as well as security and cyber architecture including and not limited to:  PKI, digital signatures.   
Background in IT/Platform covering both hardware and software networking, storage including SAN/NAS, archiving solutions, database replications (e.g., Oracle RAC/Clusterware), and the application of agile software practice for the development of reliable and repeatable automated platform deployment using tools such as CHEF.
