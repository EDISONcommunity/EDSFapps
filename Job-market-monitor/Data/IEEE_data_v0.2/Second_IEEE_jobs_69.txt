Lockheed Martin Advanced Development ProgramSKUNK WORKS-Lockheed Martin is looking for world-class talent in Operations Analysis to join our historic Skunk Works team located in Fort Worth. We need extraordinary people who are passionate about working on cutting-edge design or technology; comfortable with ambiguity; enjoy being part of a team; driven to learn and explore new areas; willing to think strategically and creatively with proven analytical & engineering problem-solving skillsAs a member of the Operations Analysis organization, the candidate will be performing modeling and analysis of military systems to quantify the impact of operational concepts (CONOPS), system design, payloads, weapons and tactics, and force size/mix on survivability and mission effectiveness, as well as perform analysis to support requirements definition, concept of operations evaluation, operational and cost effectiveness studies, and military utility assessment. Responsibilities will include researching military systems performance and operations, modeling military systems and scenarios in digital simulations, executing trade studies, analyzing simulation results to draw meaningful conclusions, preparing reports/briefings and presenting analysis results. This position will primarily be performing Human Systems Integration analysis for technology enhancements to both current and future aircraft systems focused on Pilot Vehicle Interface (PVI). This position will require someone with military operational experience to translate their operational knowledge into PVI enhancements to allow for pilot ease of useThe applicant will be responsible for maintaining a high level of expertise on Tactical System’s Pilot-Vehicle Interface (PVI), mission systems, and concepts of employment in realistic training environments. The applicant will work directly with customers by providing instruction via briefings and cockpit familiarization to achieve customers’ goals and LM Aeronautics’ strategic objectives; as well as assist with training CONOPS development and performance assessments for current and future capabilities. As an instructor for traveling & local cockpit simulation, the applicant will be responsible for supporting Business Development (BD) engagements with US & foreign military, political leaders, and the general public. In this position, the candidate may be required to support the Concept of Operations team, act as an ‘instructor pilot’ for our Virtual Simulation Environments, or only focus on PVI designs for current and future platforms. Must be a US Citizen, position is located at a facility that requires special access.
