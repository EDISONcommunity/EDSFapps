This is an opportunity to work on the Labs and Tech Services (L&TS) System Engineering team. L&TS a highly innovative team responsible for developing, testing and maintaining aircraft simulation systems and subsystems utilized for aircraft capability integration and weapons systems development from concept design through production and delivery. Within L&TS resides an extensive collection of systems integration and test capabilities, including low-cost rapid prototyping concept development systems, full-dome handling qualities simulations, high-fidelity multi-ship, real-time, man-in-the-loop simulation systems, Reprogramming Laboratories, and hardware-in-the-loop mission systems and vehicle systems integration venuesThis is a System Engineering position to work on the F-35 ACURL (Australia Canada United Kingdom Reprogramming Lab) Project located at Eglin Air Force Base, in Fort Walton Beach, Florida. The primary responsibility of this position is to perform as a member of the ACURL System Engineering team in performing system integration, anomaly resolution, and verification activities that are necessary to successfully maintain the ACURL technical capabilities to support customersThe system integration activities include providing technical expertise for: System Integration execution, participation in System Requirements Reviews, participant in Supplier Technical Reviews, System Configuration Management Controls, System Requirements Verification, System Performance Validation, and onsite installation, setup and acceptance testing of the ACURL systemThis position will require close coordination with ACURL Project and Technical Leadership, plus proactive engagement with L&TS simulation software, subsystem procurement, hardware development teams, and other teams both inside and outside LM Aeronautics, to ensure an effective system engineering communication interface is maintained across the different ACURL subsystems development teams. This position may require unique program security clearances that will be initiated upon selection and acceptance in filling this position. Final approval of these security clearances is dependent upon the Department of Defense (DoD) and could take an extensive amount of time to be granted. During this waiting period, the work assignments directly related to this position will be limited. Therefore, auxiliary organization work assignments will be providedApplicants selected will be subject to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
