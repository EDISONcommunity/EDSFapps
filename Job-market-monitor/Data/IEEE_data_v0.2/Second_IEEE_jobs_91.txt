Come and experience your future!The Information Technology System Architect (ITSA) will support the design, development, deployment, and support of Enterprise IT systems. Specific duties are expected to include updating and managing the F-35 ALIS IT system. The ALIS system is the integrated IT system that supports all sustainment aspects of the F-35 aircraft. The ITSA will focus on how information from the ALIS business aspect is transformed into the necessary system data ALIS interacts with, as well as with any applications using or processing that information/data. The ITSA will work with customers under an Agile Framework to identify critical business information needs and align those needs with the work being performed by the domain Systems Engineers, Interface Leads, and System Designers. The ITSA will also lead key system design aspects, including user interface design, data processing design, and algorithm design.
