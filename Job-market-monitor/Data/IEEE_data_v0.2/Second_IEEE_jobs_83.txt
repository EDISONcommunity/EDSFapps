This position reports to the senior manager of Reliability and Maintainability (R&M) Engineering on the F-35 program. In this position the candidate will be performing scientific research and complex statistical analyses on various functions of interest to the R&M teamThe successful candidate will analyze and interpret numerical data from quality and operational maintenance data sources; Will apply statistical methodology to provide information for Reliability and Maintainability Technical Performance Measures, scientific research, and statistical analysis to ascertain potential requirements for product improvement (Reliability Growth); Evaluate the sources of information, adjust and weigh raw data, and organize results into a form compatible with analysis by using computer applications or other methodsThe successful candidate will initiate and develop concise formats, and generate and present numerical information utilizing computer readouts, graphs, charts, tables, written reports or other methods; will respond quickly and accurately to analysis requests; Describe sources of information and limitations on usability; Analyze and interpret statistics to point out significant differences in relationships among sources of information; Prepare conclusions and forecasts based on data summaries; Recommend design or test methods for attaining specified operational reliability using knowledge of reliability engineering and other technologies; Advise and confer with engineers in scheduled Review Meetings while reviewing reliability findings and recommendationsThis position requires the ability to obtain a Secret Clearance**Must be a US Citizen.
