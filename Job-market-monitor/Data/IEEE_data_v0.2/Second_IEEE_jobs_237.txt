Be part of a dynamic team that provides Navy Command, Control, Communications, Computing, and Intelligence (C4I) Systems Engineering for Surface Warfare systems and full-spectrum fleet operations. MITRE San Diego is growing, and seeks candidates with exceptional leadership capabilities, technical skills, and domain expertise ready to tackle some of the Navys most pressing technical challenges.
 
The Lead Data Systems Engineer will be responsible for providing engineering expertise to develop the data and data analytics capabilities of Navy C4I systems. Relevant systems provide battlespace awareness to operational commanders through all-source intelligence fusion, situational awareness of cyberspace effects on battlespace operations, and detection of undersea systems through advanced signal processing. Key job functions include:

Assess technical maturity and analytical rigor of data analytics technologies to recommend their implementation or further development

Develop strategic plans for incremental fielding of data analytics capabilities

Prototype data analytics and machine learning systems

Capture, derive, translate into feasible system designs, verify, and validate requirements for data analytics and machine learning capabilities

Build and shape work programs to increase MITREs impact in data analytics and machine learning

Mentor junior staff in technical and soft skills capabilities
