Do you want to be the technical lead for high-impact team supporting DOJ's mission in a high visibility project?  This individual will be developing a proof of concept to modernize and streamline the analysis of globally sourced data for an exciting and dedicated new law enforcement sponsor.   The individual will develop a proof of concept for a new application, leveraging their existing requirements and CONOP development skills and leading an integrated and geographically dispersed team of developers, data scientists, and user interface specialists while working side-by-side with members of the law enforcement sponsors team.  The individual must have excellent communication skills and have experience with an incremental software development effort.  The individual will be responsible for providing technical guidance, which many include technical assessments, enterprise architecture, network design/architecture, technical documentation management for quality and completeness and providing day to day technical guidance leading to the successful delivery of proof of concept.

Development and successful delivery of a proof of concept
