Project Manager Product Discovery Program Vacature - Genmab - Utrecht | Indeed.nlGenmab is an international biotechnology company specializing in the creation and development of differentiated human antibody therapeutics for the treatment of cancer and other life threatening human diseases. Genmab’s core purpose is to improve the lives of patients by creating and developing innovative antibody products. Genmab’s vision is that by 2025, their own product will have transformed cancer treatment, and have a pipeline of knock-your-socks-off antibodies. The company was founded in 1999 and has listed branches in Denmark, the Netherlands, and the United States. Genmab employs approximately 230 people with its clinical development teams in Denmark and Princeton USA and R&D laboratories in The Netherlands.

The Role

In order to strengthen the Project Management team, Genmab is looking for an experienced Project Manager for the New Products program who will be based in Utrecht.
As Project Manager at Genmab, you will organise and coordinate all activities in the New Products program covering target identification, new product identification, lead generation and lead selection. You will report to the VP Project Management and to the VP Operations & Resource Management.

Key Accountabilities

Organise, structure and connect all research activities in the New Products program (including target identification, new product identification, lead generation up and lead selection)

Secure the generation of data packages to support project decision to initiate preclinical development (incl in vitro POC, in vivo POC, exploratory toxicity, FTO analysis, competitor analysis)

Act as the key linker between the lead scientists from target identification/ evaluation, panel generation, proof of concept generation to lead selection and characterisation

Monitor progress, identify opportunities and escalate in a timely manner when objectives may not be met

Lead the communication with the New Product Committee, external parties and other internal key stakeholders

Be accountable for overall budget and overview of program deliverables in New Products program

Lead the teams in close collaboration with the responsible scientist/experts to optimally execute the activities and facilitate decision making

Build teams and maintain team effectiveness and satisfaction to achieve its goals through the networks of people/functions represented on the team

Work with operational management to secure the required resources for project success, to influence optimal team composition, to align goals and objectives with project team members, coach and guide team members and to provide input for performance evaluation of project team members
Skills & Experience

You are an experienced project manager with excellent communication and stakeholder management skills that enjoys providing structure in a research environment

You are a natural leader who is flexible, agile and results-oriented, with strong focus on providing structure and organising the New Products program while keeping the flexibility of a research organisation

You are a builder of high performing cross-functional teams in a matrix organization and a natural connector of people

You have a "can do attitude" and have the necessary resilience and self-motivation required to make a maximum impact

Post Graduate Degree (PhD) (preferably in Life Science).

At least five years experience as a project manager in successful biotechnology or life sciences organisation

You are fluent in English and you are able and willing to travel (5%)
